CALL FOR ARTISTS

Currently accepting applications for artist in residence starting summer 2017!

Posted by Quinlan Deer on July 1, 2017

And it begins! I'm starting to take applications for the very first artist in residence at tiny apartment, my home in Montreal, Canada.

Tiny apartment is an idea that I had a few months ago to create a community of artists in Montreal. I haven't lived in this city for six years, and I recently find myself back here with a new perspective and a desire to create something meaningful for emerging artists. After having completed art school in Vancouver, BC, I found the art community to be a difficult world to break into. Now, living in a new(ish) city, I thought that the best way to find the art world and community was not to look for it, but to directly contribute to it.

My mission is to connect locals with new and upcoming artists from around the world. I want to provide a space where artists can work and exhibit their art to the public.

This project is completely experimental, as I have not yet facilitated anything like this before. It is flexible and open to shifting identity and concept, and will embrace exterior influeces.
